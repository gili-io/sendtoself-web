Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my devices?

Make sure both devices are connected to the same Wi-Fi network, then open Send To Self on both devices. They should automatically discover each other. Follow the on-screen pairing instructions to establish a secure connection.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Send To Self uses end-to-end encryption and direct device-to-device communication. Your data never passes through our servers or any cloud storage. Everything stays on your devices.

What types of content can I share?

You can share text, web links, and files between your devices. The app supports most common file types including documents, images, and more.

Do I need an internet connection?

Send To Self works on your local network, so you need to be connected to Wi-Fi. However, since the sharing happens directly between your devices, it doesn't require an active internet connection to external servers.
